Conference!

The SIAM Chicago-Area Conference will be taking place on April 5, 2025 at Northwestern University.

This year's conference will feature research presentations from both leading experts in the field of applied mathematics and graduate researchers. Additionally, we expect to offer opportunities for attendees to present short talks or posters on their own work.

Further details regarding the schedule will be coming soon.
